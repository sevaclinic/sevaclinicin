Refund & Cancellation Policy

This refund and cancellation policy outlines how you can cancel or seek a refund for a product / service that you have purchased through the Platform.

Cancellations

Cancellations will only be considered if the request is made within 3 days of placing the order. However, cancellation requests may not be entertained if the orders have already been communicated to our sellers or merchants listed on the Platform and they have initiated the shipping process, or if the product is out for delivery. In such cases, you may choose to reject the product at the doorstep.

Note: BIBEKANANDA PAL does not accept cancellation requests for perishable items like flowers, eatables, etc. Refund/replacement may be provided only if the user establishes that the quality of the delivered product is defective.

Damaged or Defective Products

In case you receive damaged or defective items, please report the issue to our customer service team. The request will be processed once the seller or merchant listed on the Platform checks and confirms the issue. This must be reported within 3 days of receiving the product.

Product Not as Shown / Not as Expected

If you feel that the product received is not as shown on the site or does not meet your expectations, you must notify our customer service within 3 days of receiving the product. After reviewing your complaint, the customer service team will take an appropriate decision.

Manufacturer Warranty Products

For items with a manufacturer warranty, you are requested to contact the manufacturer directly for support, repair, or replacement as applicable.

Refund Timelines

If a refund request is approved by BIBEKANANDA PAL, it will take up to 7 days for the refund to be processed to you.
