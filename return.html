Return & Exchange Policy

We offer refunds and exchanges within the first 7 days from the date of your purchase. If 7 days have passed since your purchase, you will not be offered a return, exchange, or refund of any kind.

Eligibility for Return or Exchange

To become eligible for a return or an exchange:

  • The purchased item should be unused and in the same condition as you received it.
  • The item must have its original packaging.
  • If the item was purchased during a sale, it may not be eligible for return or exchange.
  • Only items found to be defective or damaged will be replaced (based on an exchange request).

Non-Returnable / Exempt Items

You agree that certain categories of products/items may be exempted from returns or refunds. Such product categories will be identified to you at the time of purchase.

Inspection and Processing

For accepted exchange/return requests, once your returned product/item is received and inspected by us, we will send you an email to notify you about receipt of the returned/exchanged product. If the returned item is approved following our quality check, your return or exchange will be processed in accordance with our policies.

Refunds

If your return is approved, refunds (if applicable) will be processed as per our Refund & Cancellation Policy. Refunds will generally be issued to the original mode of payment and may take time to reflect depending on your bank or payment provider.

How to Initiate a Return/Exchange

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team via the contact details on the Platform with:

  • Order number
  • Details of the item(s) you wish to return/exchange
  • Reason for return
  • Photos of the item, if damaged or defective

Note: We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet the eligibility criteria.
